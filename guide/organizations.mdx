Syntaxia is multi-tenant. Every user belongs to one or more organizations, and each organization has its own source systems, AI providers, and Anchor connection. Organizations support three roles: owner (full control), admin (manage sources and AI), and member (basic access). You can invite teammates with a join code in XXXX-XXXX-XXXX format.
