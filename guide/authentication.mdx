---
title: "Authentication"
description: "How passwordless authentication works in Syntaxia."
---

Syntaxia uses passwordless authentication through magic link codes. When you sign in, you enter your email and receive a 6-character verification code. Codes use uppercase letters (excluding O, I, L) and digits 2 through 9 to avoid ambiguity. Each code expires after 15 minutes. Your session is tied to your browser and your identity (email address).
