---
title: "Ontology discovery"
description: "AI-powered analysis that discovers the meaning in your CRM data."
---

Ontology discovery uses a multi-agent debate protocol to analyze your CRM data. Specialized agents (Explorer, Micro-Critics, Arbiter, Confidence Calibrator) examine your data across eight quality dimensions over up to three debate rounds. The result is a structured L2 ontology representing the concepts, relationships, and meaning in your source system.
