---
title: "Source card scoping"
description: "How source card actions change based on your function and role."
---

Source cards display differently depending on your function and role. In Data Ops, source cards show action icons for resyncing, reprocessing, ontology discovery, reconnecting, and disconnecting. In all other functions, source cards are read-only status indicators with no action icons. Within Data Ops, admins and owners can trigger reprocess and ontology discovery, while members can trigger resync, reconnect, and disconnect.
