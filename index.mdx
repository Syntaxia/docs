Syntaxia is the operating system for business meaning. It helps RevOps teams reconcile fragmented CRM data into a single source of truth.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Sign in with a passwordless magic link. No passwords to manage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your organization">
    Create your organization and invite your team with a join code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your CRM">
    Connect Salesforce or HubSpot as a source system through OAuth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Discover your ontology">
    Run AI-powered analysis to discover the concepts and relationships in your data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose across sources">
    Align ontologies from multiple sources into unified go-to-market truth.
  </Step>
</Steps>
